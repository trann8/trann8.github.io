🚨 Business Problem:

The company is facing two critical operational challenges that threaten Q1 targets and long-term growth:



Systemic Traffic Decline: While sales revenue dropped by 7.79%, the daily order count dropped even more sharply by 9.2%. This discrepancy indicates that the primary driver of revenue loss is a failure in customer traffic/retention, not a reduction in spending power.



Regional Market Collapse (Sylhet): The Sylhet region is experiencing a 21% performance contraction, the steepest among all divisions. This is compounded by extreme demand volatility—specifically a collapse in the "Coffee Ecosystem" (causing dead stock) contrasting with a 113% surge in "Home Goods" (risking stockouts).



✅ Recommendations:

To address these issues, I recommend three key strategies based on the diagnostic analysis:



* Optimize Supply Chain (Inter-Store Transfer): Immediately freeze coffee imports for Sylhet and transfer existing excess inventory to Dhaka (where the category is still growing) to prevent expiration and waste.
* Pivot Marketing Strategy: Shift marketing spend from volume discounts (e.g., Buy 2 Get 1) to "Traffic-Driving" initiatives (e.g., Free Coffee with Visit) to directly address the 9.2% drop in daily orders.
* Conduct Rapid Operational Audit: Deploy an operations team to Sylhet for an immediate on-site assessment to identify external factors (competitors, infrastructure) driving the abnormal 21% decline.
